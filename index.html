Boise Ranch Golf Course is an 18-hole championship-style course, offering a challenging yet fair test of golf for players of all skill levels. With undulating greens, eight lakes and ponds, the 6,611-yard, par 71 layout includes one of the longest par 5s in the state of Idaho.

Experience the Ranch

Wide open fairways, rolling terrain, and the rugged beauty of Idaho. Boise Ranch offers a unique golf experience where tradition meets the modern game.
